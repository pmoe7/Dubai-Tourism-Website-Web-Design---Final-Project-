Pierchic

Seafood

Pierchic is a high-end Mediterranean seafood restaurant. Located at the end of the pier at Al Qasr, Madinat Jumeirah, the restaurant offers an over-the-water dining experience unmatched anywhere in Dubai.

Open for lunch and dinner, Pierchic features à la carte menus of classics including oysters, scallops, sashimi and lobster, as well as an array of fresh fish.

Pierchic has been redesigned to offer a modern, stylish and elegant dining space, which maximises the views of the Arabian Gulf, the Dubai coastline, Palm Jumeirah and the iconic Burj Al Arab.

Two new pods have been created along the pier leading up to the restaurant, one of which offers a full service bar – Pierchic Bar - while the other - Pierchic Lounge - is also available for private hire. Both enable guests to extend their experience at Pierchic with pre- or post-dinner drinks and will be the place to be seen in Dubai.

Pierchic also offers superior personalised service providing the perfect spot for a very special occasion.

Note that terrace seating requests are not guaranteed and operate in a first-come first-served basis.

Khaymat Al Bahar

Lebanese

Discover Lebanon's rich culinary scene every day of the week at Khaymat Al Bahar Restaurant. Open for lunch and dinner, it promises an authentic Lebanese experience with live entertainment in the evenings.

By night, the beachfront becomes the perfect spot to share a traditional mezza and shisha with friends or family in a lively and casual setting.

In addtion to our A la carte menu, you can choose amongst three set menus that include a premium choice of Arabic grills, tempting appetizers and our original Lebanese grapes and beverages. End the perfect feast on a sweet note with a selection of succulent desserts with the best beach side view in Dubai.

BiCE Mare

Italian

WINNER of What’s On Magazine’s Award in Best Seafood Restaurant category 3 years in a row, BICE Mare offers a contemporary fine dining experience. We serve Italian & seafood inspired cuisine in the heart of downtown Dubai.

Our jam packed terrace offers a breathtaking view at the iconic Burj Khalifah & grand Dubai Mall fountain show day & night. Our kitchen is run by the well-known Chef Leonardo Maltese & his talented Italian kitchen team leaving guests immersed in the most pleasurable dining experience at every visit.

BiCE Mare, is yet another award winning restaurant under the helm of the BICE Group with over 35 restaurants spanning the globe.

Pai Thai

Thai`

If the best dining experiences are a journey of discovery, Pai Thai restaurant ranks particularly highly. Your evening begins with an abra boat taking you to the restaurant, and once seated you’ll enjoy the views across Madinat’s serene waterways.

And then the culinary magic begins. Light, aromatic and invigorating dishes burst with the traditional flavours of Thailand. Choose from starters like our sundried crispy beef with coriander, garlic and chilli sauce, or mains like river prawns with creamy red curry sauce, kaffir lime leaves and red chill.

Flavour some dishes and breathtakingly romantic surroundings make an evening at Pai Thai a very special occasion. It’s little surprise this restaurant has won The Time Out Dubai Best South East Asian Restaurant Award four years running, making it a must-try Thai restaurant in Dubai.
